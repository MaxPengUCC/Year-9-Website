Hunger and Poverty


Hunger is a huge problem that affects 690 million people around the world. On this website you'll find an abundance of information about hunger and how you can help!


Donate

Hunger Facts

  1. Over 9 million people die of hunger each year worldwide

  2. Hunger can cause many chronic diseases such as high blood pressure, diabetes, and even heart disease

  3. The amount of people experiencing hunger world-wide has declined from 15% in the 2000s to 8.9% in 2018. We are making lots of progress to hopefully put an end to this crisis, but we still have long ways to go.

How Donating Helps

Donating can help in many ways towards people in need and suffereing from hunger including:

  1. All the money that is donated to non-profit organizations all go directly to help stop hunger

  2. Organizations help provide people with food, and maybe even help develop ways to make sure that people can consistently get food.
